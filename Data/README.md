Quizzes are designed to reinforce understanding by helping learners actively review and apply the concepts they have studied. Instead of only reading or watching materials passively, quizzes encourage critical thinking, problem-solving, and memory retention through practice. They also help identify areas that may require additional review, allowing students to strengthen their knowledge step by step. Regular quizzes improve confidence, promote active engagement, and support long-term learning by transforming information into practical understanding.
